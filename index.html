FAV EPISODES

1. Dinner Party (Season 4, Episode 13)

A painfully awkward yet incredibly hilarious episode that showcases Michael and Jan’s chaotic relationship.

2. Stress Relief (Season 5, Episodes 14-15)

Features some of the most iconic moments in the series, including Dwight’s fire drill and the unforgettable CPR training scene.

3. Safety Training (Season 3, Episode 20)

Michael attempts to outshine the warehouse safety training by delivering a dramatic "safety lesson" to the office staff.

4. Casino Night (Season 2, Episode 22)

An unforgettable episode where Jim confesses his love for Pam. Meanwhile, Michael invites two dates to the same event, leading to inevitable chaos.

5. The Dundies (Season 2, Episode 1)

Michael hosts his own office awards ceremony, leading to one of the funniest and most entertaining moments in the series, especially when Pam gets drunk and has the time of her life.
